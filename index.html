About MANIIRAM INDUSTRIES

Established in 1990, MANIIRAM INDUSTRIES is a trusted manufacturer and supplier of electricity distribution line materials based in Madurai, Tamil Nadu.

We specialize in manufacturing high-quality electrical hardware used by Electricity Boards, EPC contractors, infrastructure companies and power distribution utilities.

Vision

Empowering progress with reliable, high-quality electricity distribution materials built with precision and trust.

Mission

Deliver durable electrical products, support infrastructure growth and become South India's trusted distribution hardware manufacturer.

Our Products

We manufacture and supply high-quality electrical distribution line materials for utilities, contractors and infrastructure projects.

AB Switch

Cross Arms
